- pins are BROADCOM or BOARD
  - e.g.
    - broadcom: jumping around (GPIO14, GPIO15, GPIO18, GND)
    - physical: 1-40

* PCA9685
| connection | color  | BROADCOM pin | BOARD pin | PI4J pin |
|------------+--------+--------------+-----------+----------|
| GND        | BLACK  | GND          |         9 | GND      |
| SCL        | GREEN  | I2C SDL      |         5 | 9        |
| SCA        | YELLOW | I2C SDA      |         3 | 8        |
| VCC        | RED    | 3.3V         |        17 | 3.3V     |

* IRL
- stage left
- looking from the back to the front IRR is the one on the left
| connection | color | BROADCOM pin | BOARD pin | PI4J pin |

| GND        | BLACK | GND          |         6 | GND      |
| OUT        | BROWN | 14           |         8 | 15       |
