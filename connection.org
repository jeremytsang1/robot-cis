- pins are BROADCOM or BOARD
  - e.g.
    - broadcom: jumping around (GPIO14, GPIO15, GPIO18, GND)
    - physical: 1-40

* PCA9685
| connection | color  | BROADCOM pin | BOARD pin |
|------------+--------+--------------+-----------|
| GND        | BLACK  | GND          |         9 |
| SCL        | GREEN  | I2C SDL      |         5 |
| SCA        | YELLOW | I2C SDA      |         3 |
| VCC        | RED    | 3.3V         |        17 |

* L298N
| connection | color  | BROADCOM pin | BOARD pin | description   |
|------------+--------+--------------+-----------+---------------|
| GND        | BLACK  |          GND |        34 |               |
| IN1        | YELLOW |           12 |        32 | left back     |
| IN2        | GREEN  |           16 |        36 | left forward  |
| IN3        | YELLOW |            5 |        29 | right back    |
| IN4        | GREEN  |            6 |        31 | right forward |

* IRL
- stage left
- looking from the back to the front IRR is the one on the left
| connection | color | BROADCOM pin | BOARD pin |
|------------+-------+--------------+-----------|
| VCC        | RED   | 3.3V         |         1 |
| GND        | BLACK | GND          |         6 |
| OUT        | BROWN | 14           |         8 |

* IRR
- stage right
- looking from the back to the front IRR is the one on the right
| connection | color | BROADCOM pin | BOARD pin |
|------------+-------+--------------+-----------|
| VCC        | RED   | 5V           |         4 |
| GND        | BLACK | GND          |        20 |
| OUT        | BROWN | 15           |        10 |
